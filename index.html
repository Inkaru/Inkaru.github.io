Since October 2021, I am a PhD student under the supervision of Maud Marchal, Marie Babel and Claudio Pacchierotti in the RAINBOW team at the IRISA laboratory in Rennes.
My work is focused on multi-actuator vibrotactile devices and takes part in the DORNELL project, the aim of which is to design an innovative, multisensory, multimodal and intelligent haptic handle to aid mobility for disabled people. My main research interests include Haptic feedback, 3D interactions and Virtual Reality.
